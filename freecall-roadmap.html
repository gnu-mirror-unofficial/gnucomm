GNU Free Call Roadmap

Preliminary roadmap for 2011-2012

Milestones

  • Announcement March 14th 2011.
  • First phone GUI proposal done.
  • Adopt Friendika for social networking in GNU Telephony and work on extending it to offer real-time secure voice and video with GNU SIP Witch and ZRTP enabled clients.
  • First phone GUI done.
  • Web configuration interface done.
  • GNU SIP Witch plugin for Friendica done.
  • Adopt Moodle for education in GNU Telephony and work on extending it to offer real-time secure voice and video with GNU SIP Witch and ZRTP enabled clients.
